What’s new in 2026 — and why

Version 26.01 is a full rewrite, reframed from “A Balanced Perspective” to “A Call for AI Literacy.” We revise this guidance as the technology and the research evolve, so it stays current and grounded in what Arizona educators and students are actually experiencing. This edition centers AI literacy and human agency, expands the ethical considerations, and points teaching toward redesign rather than restriction. If you read an earlier version, this one is worth a fresh look; if you’re new to it, start with your role’s priority pages below — and plan to read the whole thing over time.
